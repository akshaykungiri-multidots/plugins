A Global Leader in Energy Control Solutions

Woodward is a global leader in the design, manufacture and service of energy conversion and control solutions for aerospace and industrial equipment. Our purpose is to design and deliver energy control solutions our partners count on to power a clean future.

Our innovative fluid, combustion, electrical, propulsion and motion control systems perform in some of the world’s most challenging environments, from airplanes and power plants to marine, refineries, data centers and mines. Every day our members have a meaningful impact on the industries we serve and the communities where we live.

Powered by a long legacy of innovation

Our members are helping build the future of energy control for aerospace, marine and on-highway transportation, power generation, petrochemical and other industrial sectors.

Our company was founded in 1870, focused on a breakthrough energy system of the time: waterwheels. The Woodward Governor, invented by the company’s founder Amos Woodward, was vital to some of the biggest infrastructure projects of that time. Today, our teams around the globe continue to tackle some of the biggest challenges faced by our customers and the world, from high-performing low-carbon flight to preparing industries for the transition to renewable energy.

Our relentless focus on technological leadership and decades-long collaboration with giants in aerospace and industry is accelerating development of solutions that will serve for generations to come.

Potential Suppliers

Code of Conduct

Woodward looks for suppliers who share our values and are prepared for growth. Our supplier partners create value and provide solutions for the challenges we face while collaborating and engaging in early supplier involvement during our product development process. Woodward is looking for suppliers who share our focus on quality and performance, with the ability to meet industry standards (ISO, AS) to ensure the best possible product is received by our customers. If you are interested in becoming a Woodward supplier, please click here to apply. Please have your company’s DUNS number, NAICS code, and contact information available.
