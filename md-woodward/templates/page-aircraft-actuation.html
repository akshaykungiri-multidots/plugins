Hydraulic Actuation

We have been the industry-recognized expert in design, development and manufacture of hydraulic primary, secondary and utility flight control actuators for commercial and military aircraft and rotorcraft for more than 70 years.

For decades, we have pioneered actuation solutions, from hydromechanical versions on early Douglas and Boeing jet transports to multi-redundant Fly-by-wire (FBW) actuators for current military fighter aircraft.
Our flight control actuators power flight-critical control surfaces on the most advanced aircraft with high performance and high reliability in demanding operational environments.
