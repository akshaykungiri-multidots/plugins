Storyful is the world’s leading news and intelligence agency

Founded by pioneering journalists as the first social media newswire, Storyful was created out of the need to break the news faster and edit social content to add vital context to news reporting. Acquired by News Corp in 2013, Storyful has evolved into a premium news, video and strategic intelligence partner for media, business leaders and marketing and communications professionals.

Our Mission

Storyful’s mission is to provide trusted, cross-platform content and context in a complex online information environment.For news clients, we are the definitive source for eyewitness news and viral videos, playing an integral role in newsrooms around the world. We pursue verifiable facts with the aim of providing deep context, and breaking news first.
Storyful Intelligence delivers bespoke insights derived from unique access to cross-platform data and pioneering technology, contextualized by expert analysts. We translate online societal trends and decipher digital behaviors to inform strategic brand planning and mitigate reputational risk.
